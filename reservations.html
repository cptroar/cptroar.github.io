Reservations

Visit Copenhagen Street Food – Copenhagen’s new dinning hall with 35 different stalls. Taste more than 50 different specialties from the global kitchen. Everyone is welcome – with or without table reservations.

The association that runs the new food market in Copenhagen, focus on a folksy experience. Together with friends, family and colleagues you can explore different colorful street kitchens, food trucks, containers and bars.

TABLE RESERVATIONS

more...

1. You book your table via Copenhagen Street Foods website for groups of up to 10 people.
– Are you more than 10, please contact our booking department.

2. You can make reservations every day from 17.00 during the winter season in a special area. On Sundays there is free seating in both halls.

3. When you arrive, you will be seated by a waiter. You have the table for 2 hours.

4. The waiter gives you an overview of the food stalls and takes your order for drinks.

5. You take a tour around the food stalls and buy the dishes you want to eat. – If you are a group of people we recommend to share some dished, so you taste different food. 6. Remember to bring cutlery at the food stands, unless you want to eat with your fingers – which is allowed here.

7. Then you will return to your table, where the waiter made ​​sure your glass is filled. - You pay the beverage directly to the waiter.
